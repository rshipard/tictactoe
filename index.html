Tic Tac Toe

Would you like to play a game? Do you want that game to be Tic Tac Toe? Do you have a friend, family member, imaginary friend or kidnap victim who will play with you? Great, because if you don't, whis is not the page for you!

The rules are simple, taking turns click on a square. You can't click on a square someone has already filled in. If you can get 3 squares in a row your colour, you win.
